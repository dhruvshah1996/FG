About US

What is FoodGram ?

Foodgram is a web application that serves to reduce or curb food wastage, in turn reducing the amount of food ending up in the landfills which directly impacts the release of greenhouse gases by reducing the same. It strives to do so by allowing customers to buy and/or donate leftover food items or groceries from their nearby restaurants.

Our Goal

There is about a 1/2 pound of food per meal is wasted across the restaurants in the US we are working towards figuring out a way to stop that from happening. There are pre made meals that are frozen overnight that goes to waste because the restaurants did not have the volume during the dinner hours that they were looking for so all that left over frozen food gets thrown out. We are asking the restaurants to work with us to stop that from happening.

What we do ?

We talk to the restaurants to sell that food on our website at a discounted price, where people can go on our website and buy the food at a discounted price. We are also asking volunteers to help us pick up the food and deliver it to the people that ordered the food.
